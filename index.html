Artist Statement

The aim of my work is to encourage the wearer to play with the accessories, exploring possible shapes and positions. The inspiration of my work comes from kaleidoscopes, architecture, magic balls and fractal patterns as well as natural forms. Some of work are derived from the parametric design and the work of Zaha Hadid. I am interested in products that are changeable and move mechanically. Besides thisAdditionally, I hope my designs promote playful and inquisitive feelings emotions for the wearer. The main focus of my work is to integrate mechanical movements with natural forms which evoke feelings and create an emphatic visual effect due to contrasting colours.

I work with resin, plastic and silver as my materials due to their lightness in weight. Every series uses one element as a component. To expand the wearers interaction with my work, I try to create jewellery that its function can be transformed allowing one piece of jewellery to be worn in different ways and on different parts of the body.
